How to Make Perfect Boiled Eggs

Simple, quick, and nutritious!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover them with cold water.
  2. Bring the water to a rolling boil over high heat.

  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and simmer:

    • Soft-boiled: 4-5 minutes
    • Medium-boiled: 6-7 minutes
    • Hard-boiled: 9-12 minutes
  4. Remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and place them in ice water for 5 minutes to cool.
  5. Peel and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Add a pinch of salt to the water to prevent cracking.
  • Gently tap the eggs on a hard surface and roll them to peel easily.
